Agones is an annual interdepartmental sports fest organized by the RSC (Recreation and Sports Committee) at our university. It is a vibrant and exciting event that brings together students from various departments to compete in a wide range of sports activities and competitions.

The event showcases the spirit of teamwork, sportsmanship, and healthy competition among students. Agones features a diverse lineup of sports, including basketball, football, volleyball, badminton, track events, and more. Participants have the opportunity to demonstrate their athletic abilities, forge new friendships, and foster a sense of unity within the university community.

Organized with meticulous planning and dedication, Agones is a highlight of the academic year, fostering a sense of camaraderie and promoting physical well-being. It encourages students to engage in both friendly rivalry and mutual support, fostering an atmosphere of enthusiasm and energy throughout the campus.

SPORTS OFFERED

ATHLETICS

The RNSIT Athletics Event is a dynamic showcase of sportsmanship, featuring running, relays, shotput, long jump, and more. It unites athletes in spirited competition and celebrates their athletic prowess.

BADMINTON

At RNSIT, we boast three state-of-the-art synthetic badminton courts. These courts provide an exceptional playing surface, enhancing gameplay and enabling students to refine their skills in a professional sporting environment.

FOOTBALL

At RNSIT, our vibrant football culture thrives. Talented teams compete fiercely on our grounds, battling for victory between the goal posts, fostering skill, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

KABADDI

Kabaddi ignites at RNSIT. Students engage in intense bouts of strategy and agility on our court, epitomizing teamwork and determination, fostering a spirited atmosphere of competition.

BASKETBALL

RNSIT boasts a dedicated basketball court, where students engage in competitive matches, refining their skills and fostering teamwork. The court serves as a hub for athleticism and camaraderie.

CHESS

At RNSIT, chess is more than a game—it's a mental exercise. Our students engage in strategic battles on the board, honing critical thinking and tactical skills.

CRICKET

RNSIT's cricket passion shines on our grounds. Students showcase their cricketing prowess, fostering teamwork and sportsmanship while enjoying the thrill of the game.

CROSSFIT

CrossFit at RNSIT offers diverse challenges—push-ups, ladder drills, jumps, tire pulls, and battle ropes. Students engage in high-intensity workouts, enhancing strength, endurance, and fitness levels.

POWER LIFTING

Power-lifting thrives at RNSIT. Students lift, squat, and bench with determination, pushing their limits in our dedicated arena, building strength, resilience, and camaraderie.

TABLE TENNIS

Table tennis spins at RNSIT. Students rally with precision and speed, showcasing their agility and strategy on our tables, fostering a dynamic atmosphere of competition and camaraderie.

THROWBALL

RNSIT's throwball courts echo with excitement. Students serve, spike, and dive, combining skill and teamwork, fostering a spirited atmosphere of competition and camaraderie on the campus.

VOLLEY BALL

At RNSIT, volleyball takes center stage. Players set, spike, and block, creating a dynamic atmosphere of teamwork and competition on our courts, fostering camaraderie and athletic excellence.
