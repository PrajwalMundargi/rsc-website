RNS Institute of Technology, familiar as RNSIT was established in the year 2001. It is the brainchild of Dr. R. N. Shetty, well known industrialist, and a great philanthropist. The institution was established with a sole purpose of imparting quality education in Engineering, Technology and Management. Being one of the premier engineering colleges in the country RNSIT is marching towards placing itself in the global map by excelling in academics and placements.

RNSIT SPORTS CLUB

Namma Sports Club

The RSC (College Sports Club) serves as the official hub for all sporting activities within our college community, comprising two integral wings: the Promotional Activity Wing, which orchestrates various sports-related events and initiatives, and the Sports Wing, responsible for overseeing interdepartmental competitions and recreational activities. In addition to fostering a culture of physical fitness and sportsmanship within our college, RSC also takes pride in representing our institution at the VTU Tournaments and organizing fundraising events like the "Step Up 5K Run" to support our sports-related initiatives. Our mission is to cultivate a thriving sports community that values excellence, inclusivity, sportsmanship, and unity, with the vision of becoming a renowned sports club that not only excels in college sports but also promotes an active and healthy lifestyle among students. Join us in this exciting journey towards a more vibrant and active college experience!

Vision:

To bring the best in physical education and sports to stakeholders of RNSIT.

Mission:
  1. To train students with the best infrastructure and equipment in various sports and games.
  2. To bring about awareness among students regarding physical fitness and health.
  3. To encourage students to participate in intra/inter-collegiate and university/zonal and national level sports and games.
  4. To organize sports and games for all levels at the institution.
